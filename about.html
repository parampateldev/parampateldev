Our Story

Passionate about bringing authentic Indian flavors to your table

From Family Recipes to Your Table

Nivas Kitchen was born from a deep love for authentic Indian cuisine and a desire to share the rich culinary traditions of India with our community. Founded in 2015 by Chef Nivas Patel, our kitchen has been serving the finest Indian dishes for over 8 years.

Our journey began in a small family kitchen where traditional recipes were passed down through generations. Today, we continue to honor those same time-tested techniques while embracing modern culinary innovations to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Every dish we prepare tells a story - of spices carefully selected from the finest markets, of techniques perfected over decades, and of the love and care that goes into every single meal we serve.

Meet Chef Nivas Patel

Head Chef & Founder

With over 15 years of culinary experience, Chef Nivas brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to Nivas Kitchen. Trained in traditional Indian cooking techniques from a young age, he has worked in some of the finest restaurants across India and the United States.

Chef Nivas specializes in North Indian cuisine, with particular expertise in Mughlai dishes, tandoori preparations, and traditional curries. His innovative approach to classic recipes has earned him recognition in the culinary community and the love of countless satisfied customers.
